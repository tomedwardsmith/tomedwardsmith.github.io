Pre-erected Bell Tent & Tipi Glamping

We've partnered with The Tipi & Bell Tent Hire Company to give you the option of having someone else put up a rather nice tent for you in advance!

  • Their 4m luxury, quality cotton canvas bell tents cost £180, sleeps 2-4 people and is furnished with memory foam mattresses (double or single mattress configurations are available), colourful rugs, throws, cushions, fairy lights, a low table and lamp.
  • Their 5m bell tent sleeps up to 6 people, costs £220 and is decorated as above.
  • Their 6m party bell tent sleeps 8 people and costs £300. These beautiful tents are decorated with comfy memory foam mattresses covered in throws that double up as seating, cushions, rugs, low tables, lamps and fairy lights.
  • Bedding packages cost £25 for a single quilt, sheet and pillow set, £30 for a double set or £20 for a sleeping bag and pillow set.
  • Your tent will be ready on the day with a name tag attached.
  • They also do massive tipis if you want to go all out!

If you're interested in glamping please let us know via the RSVP form. We will then send instructions on how to book to the interested parties. (Note: we do need to hit a minimum order of 8 tents for it to be possible, which is why we need to gauge interest first).
